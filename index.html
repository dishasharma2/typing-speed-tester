How It Works

  1. Select your desired test duration from the dropdown at the top.
  2. Start typing the displayed paragraph in the input area below.
  3. Real-time stats and progress bar help you track your speed and accuracy.
  4. Use the restart button anytime to reset the test and try a new paragraph.

Why Use This App?

  • Practice for coding and typing tests with real-time feedback.
  • Improve your typing accuracy and speed progressively.
  • Challenge yourself and compete with your personal best scores.
  • Enjoy a clean, responsive, and accessible user interface.
